Appearance

(GFAG/DFCG/JB/Paint.net SIFWN) Garrett appears as a poorly modeled human, he has short yellow hair, a long nose, big-ish eyes, and a mouth made of text. He wears a green shirt, light blue pants, and red shoes. He has short arms, long legs, a ball-shaped head, and a small-ish body. He sometimes has a single finger on his right hand.

(DFAC/DSHA) This is Garrett appears as a poorly modeled obese human, he has short yellow hair, a long nose, big-ish eyes, and a mouth made of text. He wears a green shirt, light blue pants, and red shoes. He has a large body. Text folats next to his head that says "THIS IS GARRETT".

(MBIBLM) Jumpman appears as a poorly modeled human, he has short yellow hair, a very long, thin nose, small eyes, and a huge mouth made of text. He wears a green shirt and light blue pants. He has long arms, long, very skinny legs, a ball-shaped head, and a somewhat large body.

(Paint 3D SIFWN) JumpMan25 appears as a poorly modeled human, he has short yellow hair, a long, thick nose, big eyes, and a mouth made of text. He wears a green shirt, blue pants, and brown shoes. He has long arms, short legs, a huge, ball-shaped head, and a round body. He has a five fingers on each hand.

(3D Movie Maker SIFWN) JumpMan25 appears as a poorly modeled human, he has a little bit of yellow hair on the top of his head, a long, thick nose, big eyes, and a big mouth made of text. He wears a green shirt. He has short arms, a wide, ball-shaped head, and a small body. He appears to be balding due to how little hair he has.

Trivia

  • Garrett is "the best person in the world".
  • Garrett owns both a Playhouse and a School.
  • Garrett started out as a fat kid who was just a student in Dave's School before leaving to start his Playhouse.

  • Garrett changes his name a lot to avoid police for cannibalism charges.
  • Garrett's best friend is PLAY_TIME_2_0.

  • Garrett is a cannibal.

  • Garrett is friends with almost everyone.
