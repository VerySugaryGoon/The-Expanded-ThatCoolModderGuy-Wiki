Aliases

Marcello, Mark, Markcello, Bambi.

Appearance

Marcello appears as a stubby humanoid. He has stubs for legs, no arms, and a head the same size as his body. His eyes are large. He wears a green hat, a red shirt, and blue pants.

Trivia

  • Marcello is hot headed.
  • Marcello has apparently broken his couch before.
  • Marcello is overweight.
  • Marcello gets extremely annoyed when he is called Bambi.
  • Marcello and Dave are good pals, but due to his hot headed personality, most don't get along with him.

  • Marcello is based off of well-known Baldi's Basics modder "Marcello_TIMEnice30".
  • Marcello is a farmer.
  • Marcello is prone to outbursts, usually ones related to anger.
  • Marcello's house isn't actually a funhouse, he simply calls it that so people will come to his house.
