"The Hungry Man" (or just "THM") would have been the It's a Bully replacement in the unfinished Baldi's Basics mod known as "Kadens Fun House Of Objects!"(/"KFHOO").

Aliases

The Hungry Man, THM, Hungry Man.

Appearance

The Hungry Man appears as a poorly modeled human, he has short brown hair, almost googly eye looking eyeballs, and a wide open mouth with red lips. He wears a red shirt and light blue paints. Text reading "IM VERY HUNGRY" floats next to his upper right.
