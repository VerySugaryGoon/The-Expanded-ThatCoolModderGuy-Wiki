Aliases

Michaels Cousin, MC, Cousin, Nike kid.

Appearance

(TCMGBIMAE) Michaels Cousin appears as a poorly modeled human with mostly normal proportions, he has a long nose, brown hair, and a mouth made out of text. He wears black shoes, black shorts, yellow and green socks, and a light blue shirt with the Nike logo on it.

(TCMG2) Michaels Cousin appears as a poorly modeled human with mostly normal proportions, he has a long nose, brown hair, and a mouth made out of text. He wears white shoes, white shorts, blue and pink socks, and a dark red shirt with the Nike logo on it.
