Aliases

PLAY_TIME_2_0, PT20, That Robot, Playtime 2.0, Robot Playtime.

Appearance

PLAY_TIME_2_0 appears as a poorly modeled, robot version of Playtime from Baldi's Basics. She "wears" a red dress, light blue paints, and brown shoes. Her dress has the text "this is actually paint not a dress" on it. Her would-be skin is a shiny metal. She has two big, misshapen eyes that change color, a pointy nose, a bald head, and a hinge-like mouth.

Trivia

  • PLAY_TIME_2_0's creator is unknown.
  • There never actually was a PLAY_TIME_1_0.
  • PLAY_TIME_2_0 can be upset easily.
  • PLAY_TIME_2_0 loves jump roping.
  • PLAY_TIME_2_0 hates sharp things.
  • PLAY_TIME_2_0 doesn't get along well with the real Playtime.
  • PLAY_TIME_2_0 is pals with Televisor 300 and Robo Kaden.
  • PLAY_TIME_2_0 is best friends with Garrett.
