"Mr. Smiles" (or just "Smiles") is the Playtime replacement of the "Sticky in: Fun with Numbers!"(/"SIFWN") series.

Aliases

Mr. Smiles, Smiles, MS, Smiley, Playtime.

Appearance

(Paint.net/SIG) Mr. Smiles appears as a VERY poorly drawn stick figure, he has a weirdly shaped head, a pear-shaped body, a huge smile, two tiny eyes, and long legs and arms. He sometimes has a hole in his head, three short fingers on each hand, and sometimes holds a "Smiler Egg" in his left hand. His head and body are yellow.

(Fangame) Mr. Smiles appears as a poorly drawn stick figure, he has a circle-ish shaped head, an almost cone-ish body, a big smile, two misshapen eyes, short legs, and long arms. He has three long fingers on each hand, along with a "Smiler Egg" in his right hand. His head and body are light yellow.

(Paint 3D) Mr. Smiles appears as a poorly drawn stick figure, he has a circle-ish shaped head, an almost baseball bat-shaped body, a big smile, two misshapen eyes, and long-ish legs and arms. He has three long fingers on each hand, along with a "Smiler Egg" in his left hand. His head and body are yellow.

(3D Movie Maker) Mr. Smiles appears as a poorly drawn stick figure-type character, he has a huge circular head, a pear-ish shaped body, a huge smile, two misshapen eyes, short legs, and long arms. He has three fingers on each of his hands. His head and body are yellow.

Trivia

  • Mr. Smiles has one known family member, "Sir. Smiles".
  • Mr. Smiles has progressively gotten smaller each design update.
  • Mr. Smiles is apparently a "Weird Creature" due to the fact he is allowed in the "no people that are not weird creatures ALLOWED!" rooms.
  • Mr. Smiles is a part of a monotreme species known as "Smilers".
  • Between the events of the 3D Movie Maker and Paint 3D versions of SIFWN, Mr. Smiles laid an egg.
  • Mr. Smiles can be upset very easily despite what his poster says.
  • Mr. Smiles is very close with Push N Learn.
  • Mr. Smiles is friends with EVERYONE.
