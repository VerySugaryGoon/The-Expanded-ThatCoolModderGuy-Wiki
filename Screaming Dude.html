Aliases

Screaming Dude, SD, Screaming Guy.

Appearance

Screaming Dude appears as a chibi human, he has a large head, long eyes, a huge mouth made of text, short arms and legs, and a cube-shaped body. He wears a blue shirt and green pants. He is bald.
