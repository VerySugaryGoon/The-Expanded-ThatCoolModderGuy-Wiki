"Professor Baldi Baldimore" (or just "Baldi") is the main character and antagonist of the "Baldi's Basics"(/"BB") series. He replaces Principal of the Thing/Arts and Crafters in various mods.

Appearance

(BB+/BBIEAL/BBFGPD/BBFTDC/BBBB/PWHNSLG/QBCAISIEAL/GFAG/TCMGBIMAE/TCMG2/TCMGA/DFAC/JCT/MBIBLM/FAMG/DNB/Paint 3D SIFWN/Paint.net SIFWN) Baldi appears as a poorly modeled human with one strand of brown hair atop his bald head. He has small eyes, a red-lipped mouth, a big-ish nose, and flat hands with long fingers. He wears a green shirt, blue pants, and brown shoes. His right elbow is ghost white. He is often seen wearing other outfits such as; gray shoulder pads (TCMG2), a green party hat with red spots and a blue tip (BBBB/PWHNSLG), a yellow camping hat and brown backpack (BBFTDC). In DFAC he appears quite glitchy.

(DFCG) Baldi appears as a poorly modeled human with a bald head. He has small eyes, a red-lipped mouth, a yellow nose, ball-shaped hands. He wears a green shirt, blue pants, and brown shoes.

(SIG/3D Movie Maker SIFWN) Baldi appears as a small, poorly modeled human. He has a wide head, large eyes, a red mouth, and a large brown strand of hair on his bald head. The rest of his body isn't seen.

Trivia

  • Baldi is 6'0.
  • Baldi hates the Tape Player Tipper.
  • Baldi has amazing hearing.
  • Baldi owns two schools, "Here School" and the "Super Schoolhouse".
  • Baldi gets along with Garrett and Sticky but wouldn't really call them friends.
  • Baldi loves camping.
  • Baldi likes to play hide and go seek.
  • Baldi is a math teacher.
  • Baldi is best friends with ThatCoolModderGuy and Principal of the Thing.
  • Baldi smacks people with his ruler but would never kill anyone.
  • Baldi quit his job for a while after the events of BBFGPD, but later returned to teaching.
  • Baldi likes to invent things.
  • Baldi is friends with almost everyone.
