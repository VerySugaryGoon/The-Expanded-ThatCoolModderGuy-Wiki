Appearance

God appears as an edited version of ThatCoolModderGuy. He has eight eyes, a large mouth made out of two "E"s, and huge white wings. His hair is just a floating brown brick near where the top of his head would be. He wears white pants and a yellow shirt with the text "don't be scared" on it. He lacks feet, hands, and a head.
