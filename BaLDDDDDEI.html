Aliases

BaLDDDDDEI, BaLDEI, Unnamed version Baldi.

Appearance

BaLDDDDDEI appears as an edited version of Baldi from Baldi's Basics. He is mainly seen as a highly dithered light purple, white, and black version of Baldi but other times he is seen as a white and black beveled version of Baldi. He is always seen covered in Notebooks.
