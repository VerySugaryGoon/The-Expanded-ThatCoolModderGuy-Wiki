Aliases

Paloose Man, PM, Paloose.

Appearance

Paloose Man appears as a poorly modeled overweight human with one arm. He wears a light blue shirt and hat, both with the "Paloose Fork" badge on them. His shirt also has the text; "paloose" on it. He wears blue pants and brown shoes. His expression is angry and he holds out his left hand, doing a "Stop!" gesture. Text above his hand reads; "stop sir!".
