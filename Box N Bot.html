"Box N Bot" (or just "BNB") is the 1st Prize replacement in the Paint.net version of "Sticky in: Fun with Numbers!"(/"SIFWN").

Aliases

Box N Bot, BNB, BoxerBot.

Appearance

Box N Bot appears as a poorly made Paint 3D model, he has a weirdly-shaped head with two miss-shapen on eyes and a frown, he also has some form of gray antenna with a red tip sticking out from the 'top' of his head. His body is made up of thick blue tubes. He has two chubby boxing gloves as hands each with a stubby thumb and has two short wheels that he uses to get around.

Trivia

  • Box N Bot is the strongest character in Sticky's School.
  • Box N Bot can kill almost anything in one punch.
  • Box N Bot is essentially an inverted version of 1st Prize.
  • Box N Bot is apparently a "Weird Creature" due to the fact he is allowed in the "no people that are not weird creatures ALLOWED!" rooms.
  • Box N Bot doesn't really get along with most others besides Sticky, The King Of Rules, and Professor Whatshisname.
  • Box N Bot doesn't get along with Push N Learn.
  • Box N Bot was made by Professor Whatshisname.
  • Box N Bot was made in 2019.
