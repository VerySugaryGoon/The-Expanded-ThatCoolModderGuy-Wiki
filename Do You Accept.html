Appearance

Do You Accept appears as a poorly modeled human-like demon with red eyes, stubby legs, and a long body. His hair is simply the text; "hair" placed on his head. His nose is oddly shaped, his eyebrows are thick. He has a smug expression. He wears red pants, brown shoes, and a blue shirt that has the text; "dont trust this guy!!!!!!!!!!!!" on it. He holds a cardboard box with text on it that reads; "best gift!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!". One of his eyes is much larger than the other.
