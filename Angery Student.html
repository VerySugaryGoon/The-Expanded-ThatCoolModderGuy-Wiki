Appearance

(Paint.net) Angery Student as a human-like creature made out of basic shapes. He has a circle head, nose, and eyes. His mouth and eyebrows are drawn poorly. He wears a blue shirt, green pants, and brown shoes. His hands are made out of text. He has long arms and short legs.

(Paint 3D) Angery Student as a poorly modeled human-like creature. He has a big head with wide eyes and a ball-shaped nose. His mouth and eye brows are made out of black curved cylinders. He wears a green shirt, blue pants, and brown shoes. His hands are made out of text. He has long arms and short legs.
