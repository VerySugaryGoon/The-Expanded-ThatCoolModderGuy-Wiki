"Old Ugly Stick" (or just "OUS") is the Filename2 replacement in the Paint.net version of "Sticky in: Fun with Numbers!"(/"SIFWN").

Aliases

Old Ugly Stick, Sticky's Brother, OUS.

Appearance

Old Ugly Stick appears as a poorly drawn stickman with huge eyes, one arm, and a sort of crazed expression. He appears to have extreme periorbital hyperpigmentation. His mouth and arm are made out of text.
