Aliases

Speedy, Speed, This Guy, Speed Shoes, Slow-Pokey.

Appearance

Speedy appears as a poorly modeled human, he wears a yellow shirt, green pants, and his speed shoes. He has misshapen eyes, arms, and hands. He has a large frown. His speed shoes appear as yellow shoes with a red wavy line on each shoe, each shoe also has a large red lightning bolt on the sides.

Trivia

  • Speedy both loves and hates his speed shoes.
  • Speedy is in an objectum relationship with Gotta Drive.
  • Speedy claims to be the owner of Gotta Drive, though this is likely just a joke.
  • Speedy likes to sit on top of Gotta Drive and ride around.
  • Speedy sometimes races Circle.
  • Speedy was named "Slow-Pokey" before the speed shoe incident.
  • Speedy is good friends with ThatCoolModderGuy, Circle, Professor Whatshisname, and of course Gotta Drive.
