Appearance

Circle appears as a poorly made object show-type character, he has stick figure limbs, and a main body that is a green circle. He has long eyes and a large mouth. He is heavily beveled.

Trivia

  • Circle is from Shape World.
  • Circle sometimes does races with Speedy.
  • Circle once lived in Shape World with the other shapes but left for an unknown reason.
  • Circle can reach a top speed equal to 1/10 the speed of light.
  • Circle is good friends with most of the robots and other kids.
  • Circle has a hard time with his feelings, making him very emotional.
  • Circle hates Nerf Guns and thinks they should be banned.
