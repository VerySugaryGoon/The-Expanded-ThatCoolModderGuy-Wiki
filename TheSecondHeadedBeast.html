Appearance

TheSecondHeadedBeast appears as an edited version of "Garrett", he is extremely blue, with his body being a light blue. His head is replaced by two smaller Garretts, said Garretts have a darker blue skin and have white hair, pupils, eyebrows, and mouth. Their bodies are purple-ish.
