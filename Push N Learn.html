"Push N Learn" (or just "PNL") is the 1st Prize replacement in the 3D Movie Maker and Paint 3D versions of "Sticky in: Fun with Numbers!"(/"SIFWN") mods.

Aliases

Push N Learn, PNL, 1st Prize.

Appearance

(Paint 3D/Fangame) Push N Learn appears as a poorly made Paint 3D model, he has a cube-shaped head with two poorly stuck on eyes and a large open mouth, he also has some form of green and yellow antenna sticking out from the side of his head. His body is made up of blue tubes. He has two chubby hands each with five fingers and has one large wheel that he uses to get around.

(3D Movie Maker) Push N Learn appears as a poorly made 3D Movie Maker model, he has a huge cube-shaped head with two large eyes and a large open mouth, he also has some form of green and yellow antenna sticking out from the side of his head. His main body is made up of one blue tube, and his arms appear to be springs. He has two blue balls on the end of each arm, meant to be hands, and has one large wheel that he uses to get around.

Trivia

  • Push N Learn is best friends with Mr. Smiles.
  • He speaks in zeros and ones in the 3DMM version of SIFWN.
  • Push N Learn is apparently a "Weird Thing" due to the fact he is allowed in the "weird things that are not human ONLY" rooms.
  • Push N Learn shouldn't be able to hold himself up due to only having one wheel yet he somehow does.
  • Push N Learn is the only character to appear in the Paint 3D version of SIFWN but not the Paint.net version.
  • Push N Learn doesn't get along with Box N Bot.
  • Push N Learn was made by Professor Whatshisname.
  • Push N Learn was made in 2018.
  • Push N Learn "died"( got replaced) in 2019.
