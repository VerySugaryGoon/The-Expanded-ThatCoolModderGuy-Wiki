"Super Phone" (or just "SP") is the Pay Phone replacement in the "Sticky in: Fun with Numbers!"(/"SIFWN") series.

Aliases

Super Phone, SP.

Appearance

(Paint.net) Super Phone appears as a huge cartoony phone. It's shell is green with small blue text that reads "this is a super phone!" on it, a small blue screen with a waving Sticky saying "hi!" on it, seemingly randomly sized number buttons (0-9), and a large blue diamond-shaped button with 3D text on it that says "CALL!".

(Paint 3D/Fangame) Super Phone appears as a huge, poorly modeled phone. It's shell is green with orange text that reads "token please" on it, a small white screen with a Sticky on it with a shocked expression, and seemingly randomly sized and colored number buttons (0-9).

(3D Movie Maker) Super Phone appears as a huge, poorly drawn phone. It's shell is green with red-green text that reads "Hello!" on it, a blue white screen with a Sticky waving on it, and large red button with the numbers 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 10, on it.

Trivia

  • The Super Phone is alive.
  • The Super Phone needs money for an unknown reason.
  • The Super Phone doesn't have a poster in the Paint 3D version of SIFWN for some reason.
  • It's unknown what happens when you call the number "666" on the Super Phone. It could possibly cause the user to call the odd looking Sticky on the Super Phone poster.
  • It's unknown how the Super Phone actually works due to it having no way to end a call, pay to make a call, etc.
