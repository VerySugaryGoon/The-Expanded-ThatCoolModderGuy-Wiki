"Hunger Man" (or just "HM") is the It's a Bully replacement of the "Sticky in: Fun with Numbers!"(/"SIFWN") series.

Aliases

Hunger Man, HM, Hungeryest Boy, I AM VERY HUNGRY, The creature of despair, Hungry Man, It's a Bully.

Appearance

(Paint.net) Hunger Man appears as a poorly modeled, almost plant-like creature. He has a purple stick-body, a big light blue head with an equally big eye on top, his eye and mouth are made with text, blue and purple text under his head reads; "I AM HUNGRY FEED ME STUFF", and he sits in a light red pot/holder. He always appears sad.

(Paint 3D/Fangame/3D Movie Maker) Hunger Man appears as a poorly modeled, almost plant-like creature. He has a purple stick-body, a big blue head with an equally big eye on top, his eye and mouth are made with text, dark blue and purple text under his head reads; "I AM HUNGRY FEED ME STUFF", and he sits in a red pot/holder. He always appears sad.

Trivia

  • Despite having "Man" in his name, Hunger Man is quite clearly non-human.
  • Hunger Man did not get a design update in the Paint 3D version of SIFWN.
  • Hunger Man is apparently a "Weird Creature" due to the fact he is allowed in the "no people that are not weird creatures ALLOWED!" rooms.
  • Hunger Man was at one point well-fed and was called "Hungry Man".
  • Hunger Man eats people who "just so happen" to die in Sticky's School.
  • Hunger Man and The Hungry Man get along well.
  • Hunger Man isn't friends with anyone in Sticky's school besides Mr. Smiles and Sweepy/Sweepit.
  • Hunger Man is three hundred and one years old.
