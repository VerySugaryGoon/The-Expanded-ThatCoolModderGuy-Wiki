Aliases

JumpMale, Unnamed Arts and Crafters Replacement, Arts and Crafters, The meaning of Life.

Appearance

JumpMale appears as a poorly made 3D Movie Maker model, he has a long nose, two large and sad looking eyes, and a large frowning mouth made out of text. He has a brown circle on his head, seemingly meant to be hair. He wears a red shirt, purple pants, and brown shoes. He has very thin arms but thick legs and large feet.
