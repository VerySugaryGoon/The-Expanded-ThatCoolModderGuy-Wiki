Appearance

It appears as a huge home(?) with seven bedrooms, five private rooms, an office, and a closet for Paint Roller. The ceiling is purple with red stars on it, the walls are purple and blue, and the floor is light blue and purple stripes. Private room doors are blue while all other doors are red.
