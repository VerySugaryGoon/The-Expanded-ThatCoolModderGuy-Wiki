"The King Of Rules" (or just "TKOR") is the Principal of the Thing replacement of the "Sticky in: Fun with Numbers!"(/"SIFWN") series.

Aliases

The King Of Rules, TKOR, King, The King, King Of The School, BEST GUY, Principal of the Thing.

Appearance

(Paint.net) The King Of Rules appears as a highly beveled non-human creature, he has long arms and legs, a large round main body, and a big white smiley face. He wears a small golden pyramid with the text "AM KING!" on it, on the top of his body. His whole body is black.

(Paint 3D) The King Of Rules appears as a poorly modeled non-human creature, he has long arms and legs, a large round main body, big feet, and a white frowning face. He has a unibrow and his mouth is made out of text. His whole body is black.

(3D Movie Maker) The King Of Rules appears as a poorly modeled non-human creature, he has tiny arms and legs, a large round main body, and a large white annoyed face. He has a unibrow. His mouth and left arm are made out of text. His whole body is black.

Trivia

  • The King Of Rules matured over the course of the three versions of SIFWN.
  • The King Of Rules has become happier over the course of the three versions of SIFWN.
  • His crown is fake and is actually just a ".pcx" image on his head.
  • The King Of Rules is apparently a "Weird Creature" due to the fact he is allowed in the "no people that are not weird creatures ALLOWED!" rooms.
  • The King Of Rules is a very bossy person which makes him overall unlikeable by most.
  • The King Of Rules is the tallest SIFWN character, standing at a little over 8 feet.
  • The King Of Rules isn't actually a king, in fact it's more of a roleplay if anything.
  • The King Of Rules doesn't get along with most people outside of Sticky and Principal of the Thing.
  • He has at LEAST 927 rules.
