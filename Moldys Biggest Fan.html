Aliases

Appearance

Moldy's Biggest Fan appears as a poorly modeled human-like. He has two skinny stubby legs, no arms, a wide main body, and a ball-shaped head. His mouth is made of text and his brown hair and black eyes are simply drawn on. He wears a brown tophat(?), Blue shirt, and red pants. Both his hat and shirt have images of Moldy on them.
