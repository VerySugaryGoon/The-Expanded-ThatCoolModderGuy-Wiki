Aliases

Mr. Music, Music, The Music Teacher.

Appearance

Mr. Music appears as a piano headed person. He wears black paints, an invisible/white undershirt and a black jacket with blue music notes on it. He holds a guitar. Although unseen in his sprite he wears black dress shoes. He has goofy, almost googly eye looking eyeballs.

Trivia

  • Mr. Music has a normal human voice but it is always overlayed by piano music while he speaks.
  • Mr. Music is the tallest living thing inside the Playhouse.
  • Mr. Music can play any instrument perfectly.
  • Mr. Music is "The best musician to ever live".
  • Mr. Music is in a relationship with Do You Accept.
  • Mr. Music teaches music in his free time.
  • Mr. Music views himself as the glue that holds the Playhouse together.
  • Mr. Music sees Garrett and PLAY_TIME_2_0 as his responsibility and takes somewhat good care of them.
