Aliases

Smartie, Player, Idiot.

Appearance

Smartie appears as a poorly drawn alien-robot(?) sort of entity. He has two bug-like eyes that sit atop long stems that come out of his plate-like head and an exposed brain sat ontop of a plate-like head part of his body. His lower body is a laptop while his upper body is blue with buttons and a speaker on it. He has four arms and two legs, with them being stickman-like in how they appear.

Trivia

  • Sadly due to the Paint 3D version of SIFWN being lost media, we will most likely never see his Paint 3D full-body appearance.
  • Despite being named "Smartie", he is actually somewhat stupid.
  • Smartie is still most likely the Player replacement in the 3D Movie Maker version of SIFWN despite having no assets or audio in said version.
  • Smartie is bad at math.
  • Smartie is replaced by Kid in the SIFWN Fangame.
  • Smartie is apparently not a "Weird Creature" due to the fact that he isn't allowed in the "no people that are not weird creatures ALLOWED!" rooms.
  • Smartie dislikes both Squidward and Box N Bot.
  • Smartie is friends with Mr. Smiles, Push N Learn, and The Hand Man but gets along okay with most of the cast as well.
