"Moldy" is the main character and antagonist in "Moldy's Basics In Being Le Modder"(/"MBIBLM"). He replaces Baldi.

Aliases

Moldy, Mold, MoldyGH, Le Modder.

Appearance

Moldy appears as a poorly modeled human, he has short brown hair, a large body, long arms, long skinny legs, a smug expression, and sometimes fingers and very flat shoes. He wears a blue shirt, red paints, and gray shoes. His eyes and mouth are made out of text. His appearance is very inconsistent.
