"ThatCoolModderGuy" (or just "TCMG") is the main character and antagonist of the "ThatCoolModderGuys Basics in Mods and Edits!"(/"TCMG") series. He replaces Baldi/Dave/Arts and Crafters/Principal of the Thing.

Appearance

(TCMGBIMAE) ThatCoolModderGuy appears as a poorly modeled human with normal-ish proportions, he has a long nose, a brown brick-like object on his head meant to be hair, along with a mouth made out of text. He wears black shoes, yellow pants, and a green shirt with the text "insert text here" on it.

(TCMG2) ThatCoolModderGuy appears as a poorly modeled human with normal-ish proportions, he has a long nose, a brown brick-like object on his head meant to be hair, along with a mouth made out of text. He wears white shoes, blue pants, and a purple shirt with the text "sure i guess" on it.

(TCMGA) ThatCoolModderGuy appears as a poorly modeled human with normal-ish proportions, he has a long nose, a brown brick-like object on his head meant to be hair, along with a mouth made out of text. He wears red Roller Skates, yellow pants, and a blue-sleeved green shirt with the text "insert text here" on it.

Trivia

  • ThatCoolModderGuy is friends with Jeff.
  • ThatCoolModderGuy seems to be a child despite being a teacher, homeowner, and owning a car.
  • A Paint.net version of ThatCoolModderGuy can be seen in TCMGBIMAE.
  • ThatCoolModderGuy is able to punch hard enough to make a sort of "fireball" appear for a split-second.
  • He can't spell.
  • It's unknown how he made his way into Idiot88s school.
  • ThatCoolModderGuy is friends with Idiot88 and was once even canonically with her.
  • ThatCoolModderGuy has Yeetamuss Beetamuss locked in "TCMGs ROOM" for an unknown amount of time.
  • ThatCoolModderGuy and Cayden seemingly do not get along.
  • ThatCoolModderGuy appears in all the George game outside of the second one where he is replaced by a robot version of himself.
  • Despite not being able to spell he can code/mod.
  • ThatCoolModderGuy is friends with Sticky, Garrett, and most other Baldi replacements along with Baldi himself.
