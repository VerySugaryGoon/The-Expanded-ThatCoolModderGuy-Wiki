"Kaden" is the main character and antagonist in an unfinished Baldi's Basics mod known as "Kadens Fun House Of Objects!"(/"KFHOO"). He would have replaced Baldi.

Aliases

Kaden, Kadey, Kay.

Appearance

Kaden appears as a poorly modeled human, he has short brown hair, long legs, a long pointy nose, and misshapen eyes. He wears a orange-yellow shirt, light blue paints, and red shoes. His mouth is made out of text.
