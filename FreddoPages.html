Appearance

It appears as a large school with seven classrooms, five "GOOD GUYS ONLY!" rooms, an office, and a closet for Disabled Sweep. The ceiling is a wavy blue pattern, the walls are made of balls of many colors, and the floor is bricks, also of many colors. There is a small playground outside the school with two slides and two swing-sets.
