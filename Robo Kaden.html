"Robo Kaden" (or just "RK") would have been the Playtime replacement in the unfinished Baldi's Basics mod known as "Kadens Fun House Of Objects!"(/"KFHOO").

Aliases

Robo Kaden, Robot Kaden, RK.

Appearance

Robo Kaden appears as a poorly modeled robot human, he has short brown hair, a long pointy nose, cube-shaped eyes, and a speaker for a mouth. He "wears" a orange-yellow shirt with buttons and a large speaker on it, light blue paints, and red shoes. His colors are shiny and metal.
